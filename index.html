Nathan is a San Francisco State University student majoring in Visual Communication Design. Before transferring to San Francisco State, he had attended City College of San Francisco. After a couple of years of trying out different majors at CCSF, Nathan decided to pursue Visual Communication Design. It was then that he knew he found the right major. Nathan had always had an affinity to creativity. As a child, drawing was one of the things that he had done often. Although drawing didn't seem like a stable major, he instead chose to pursue Visual Communication Design and has never looked back since.

Nathan likes to be creative during his free time, or anything that is related to creativity. Some of his hobbies that he likes to do is drawing and design. Through drawing and design, he's able to express himself and share it with others. Nathan also enjoys gaming and hanging out with friends.

This site provides a record of his digital footprint based off of some of the things that he likes and some of the things that he does.
